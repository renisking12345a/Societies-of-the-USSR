Getting Started with Military Simulator

This tutorial will guide you through the basics of Military Simulator and help you get acclimated to the game's core mechanics. We'll cover essential aspects like:

  • Unit Control and Movement
  • Combat Fundamentals
  • Jumping jacks
  • Grammar jacks
  • Hell jacks
  • Diverted jacks

Unit Control and movement.

When you control your movement you must be percise with it like always be next to some cover if you are the attacker do repeated movement you can crouch move left and right and jumping when you can if you are the one being attacked then just find some cover and always be on it it or hiding at it.

Combat fundamentals

For the combat fundamentals you will need a gun if you have 2 or more choose the one with the fastest rate of fire but with a decent amount of damage.

Jumping jacks(JJ's)

Jumping jacks are when you start counting the numbers must be all caps like this: "ONE" (jump) "TWO" (jump) and so on...

Grammar jacks(GJ's)

Grammar jacks are when you start counting the numbers must start with a capital letter and then end with a punction "."they are done like this: "One." (jump) "Two." (jump) and so on...

Hell jacks (HJ's)

Hell jacks are when you start counting the numbers must be all caps and spell every letter of the number seperately like this: "O" (jump) "N" (jump) "E" (jump) "ONE" (jump) and so on...

Diverted jacks

Diverted jacks are when you start counting the numbers backwards meaning they are like this: ".enO" (jump) ".owT" (jump) and so on...
