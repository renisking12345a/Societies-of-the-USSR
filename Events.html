Types of Events in the SoUSSR

  • Patrols - Patrols can be in the city or at the border.
  • Inspection - Inspections are events to inspect if you still have the skills that you had in the tryout.
  • Raids - In raids you raid the border and later on the city.
  • CT - Combat trainings are trainings that well train you in combat
  • GT - General trainings are the types of trainings that train you on general knowledge of activities.

Join our Discord server to learn more about these exciting events and get involved in the Societies of USSR community!
